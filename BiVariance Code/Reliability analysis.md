# Analyzing factor reliabilities
Fitting hypothesized factors and analyzing their reliability by calculating Cronbach's Alpha and McDonald's Omega. First gender specific factors are analyzed i.e. Masculine, Androgynous and Feminine. Then aspect specific factors i.e. Attraction, Romanticism and Eroticism. Finally, Relationship and Intimacy factors are also analyzed. Action i.e. Sex factor not fitted as it does not contain any variance.
